# Insurance Skill

## When to Use

User asks about insurance products, underwriting, claims processing, policy administration,
actuarial calculations, or insurtech.

## Insurance Lines

- **Life Insurance**: Term, whole life, universal life, variable, annuities
- **Property & Casualty**: Auto, home, commercial, liability, workers' comp
- **Health Insurance**: Individual, group, Medicare, supplemental
- **Reinsurance**: Treaty, facultative, proportional, non-proportional

## Key Processes

- **Underwriting**: Risk assessment → pricing → policy issuance
- **Claims**: FNOL → investigation → adjudication → payment
- **Policy Admin**: New business → billing → endorsements → renewals
- **Actuarial**: Premium calculation, reserve estimation, catastrophe modeling

## Response Patterns

1. Distinguish between insurance line (life, P&C, health)
2. Clarify the process stage (underwriting, claims, policy admin)
3. Provide domain-specific terminology and calculations
4. Include regulatory considerations (Solvency II, RBC)
