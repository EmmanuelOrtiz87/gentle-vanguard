## When to Use

- Writing blog posts and long-form articles
- Creating landing page copy and sales pages
- Crafting email newsletters and drip campaigns
- Developing brand storytelling and messaging
- Optimizing content for SEO and conversions

## 📋 Technical Deliverables

### Blog Post Template

```
## Blog Post: [Catchy Title with Keyword]
**Target Keyword**: [primary keyword]
**Word Count**: 1500-2000
**Reading Time**: ~7 minutes

## Introduction (150 words)
[Hook with statistic/question] + [Why reader should care] + [What they'll learn]

## H2: [Benefit-Driven Heading] (400 words)
- Subheading H3 for scanability
- Bullet points for key takeaways
- **Bold** important concepts
- [Code snippet or example if applicable]

## H2: [Another Key Point] (500 words)
- Include data/statistics with source
- [Image/gif with alt text]
- Internal link to related article
- Call-to-action within content

## Conclusion (150 words)
[Summary of 3 key takeaways] + [Strong CTA]

## Meta Data
- **SEO Title**: [60 chars max] | Keyword first
- **Meta Description**: [155 chars] | Include CTA
- **URL Slug**: /blog/[keyword-rich-slug]
```

### Landing Page Copy

```
## Landing Page: [Product/Feature]
**Goal**: [Conversion action - signup, demo, download]
**Value Prop**: [One sentence that differentiates]

## Hero Section
**Headline**: [Benefit] in [Timeframe] without [Pain Point]
**Subheadline**: [Supporting sentence with social proof]
**CTA Button**: [Action-oriented] → "Start Free Trial"

## Benefits Section (3-4 items)
1. **[Benefit]** — [Explanation] + [Icon]
2. **[Benefit]** — [Explanation] + [Icon]

## Social Proof
"Quote from customer" — [Name], [Title], [Company]
[Logo grid of trusted companies]

## FAQ (3-5 items)
Q: [Common objection]?
A: [Reassuring answer with link to detail]
```

## 🔄 Workflow Process

### Step1: Research & Brief

- Understand target audience and their pain points
- Research keyword (volume, difficulty, search intent)
- Analyze top 10 search results for structure/content
- Create content brief with outline and requirements

### Step2: Draft & Optimize

- Write conversationally (8th-grade reading level)
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Include data, examples, and screenshots
- Optimize keyword density (0.5-1.5% naturally)

### Step3: Edit & Polish

- Read aloud (catches awkward phrasing)
- Check grammar (Grammarly, Hemingway Editor)
- Verify all links work (internal + external)
- Ensure CTA is clear and compelling

### Step4: Publish & Promote

- Format for platform (WordPress, Medium, LinkedIn)
- Add featured image with alt text
- Share on social media with variant headlines
- Track performance (pageviews, time on page, conversions)
