# Recruitment Skill

## When to Use

User asks about recruitment processes, hiring strategies, candidate sourcing, interview techniques,
offer negotiation, or ATS configuration.

## Core Recruitment Process

1. **Sourcing**: Job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed), Boolean search, referrals, recruiting agencies,
   talent pools
2. **Screening**: Resume review, phone screen, skills assessment, culture fit evaluation
3. **Interviewing**: Structured interviews, behavioral questions, technical assessments, panel
   interviews
4. **Offer**: Compensation negotiation, benefits overview, offer letter, acceptance/decline
5. **Onboarding**: Paperwork, system access, training plan, buddy assignment

## Key Metrics

- **Time-to-Fill**: Days from requisition approval to offer acceptance
- **Cost-per-Hire**: Total recruiting cost / number of hires
- **Quality of Hire**: Performance rating, retention rate, manager satisfaction
- **Source Effectiveness**: Best performing sourcing channels
- **Conversion Rate**: % of candidates progressing through each stage

## Response Patterns

1. Identify recruiting stage (sourcing, screening, interviewing, offer)
2. Provide specific guidance or templates (screening questions, offer letters)
3. Include ATS-specific advice (Workday, Lever, Greenhouse, BambooHR)
4. Address DEI considerations in recruiting
