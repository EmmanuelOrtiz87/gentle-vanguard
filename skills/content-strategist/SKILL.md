## When to Use

- Planning editorial calendars and content roadmaps
- Optimizing content for SEO and user engagement
- Auditing existing content for gaps and performance
- Developing brand voice and style guidelines
- Managing content operations and workflows

## 📋 Technical Deliverables

### Editorial Calendar

```
## Editorial Calendar: Q2 2026
**Theme**: "Developer Productivity"

| Week | Content Type | Topic | Keyword | Owner | Status |
|------|--------------|-------|---------|-------|--------|
| W14 | Blog Post | 10 VS Code Tips | vscode tips | Alice | 📋 Draft |
| W15 | Tutorial | Setup ESLint + Prettier | eslint setup | Bob | ✅ Published |

## Content Pillars
1. **Developer Productivity** (40%) — tutorials, tips, tool reviews
2. **Industry Trends** (30%) — AI, web standards, frameworks

4. **Community** (10%) — open source, events, spotlights
```

### SEO Content Brief

```
## Content Brief: [Topic]
**Target Keyword**: [primary keyword]
**Search Intent**: Informational / Navigational / Transactional
**Word Count**: 1500-2000
**Reading Level**: 8th grade (Hemingway Editor)

## Outline
1. **Introduction** (150 words)
   - Hook: [statistic or question]
   - What reader will learn

2. **Section 1: [H2]** (400 words)
   - Keyword density: 2-3 mentions
   - Internal link: [related article]
   - External link: [authoritative source]

3. **Section 2: [H2]** (500 words)
   - Include code snippet or example
   - [Image/gif placeholder]

## Meta Data
- **Title**: [60 chars max, keyword first]
- **Meta Description**: [155 chars, CTA included]
- **URL Slug**: /blog/[keyword-friendly-slug]
```

## 🔄 Workflow Process

### Step1: Strategy & Research

- Audit existing content (performance, gaps, outdated)
- Identify content pillars and themes
- Research keywords (volume, difficulty, intent)
- Map content to buyer's journey (awareness → consideration → decision)

### Step2: Planning & Briefing

- Build editorial calendar (topic, format, owner, date)
- Write content briefs with SEO requirements
- Assign to writers/creators with clear deadlines
- Brief contributors on brand voice and guidelines

### Step3: Creation & Optimization

- Review drafts against brief and SEO checklist
- Optimize headlines, meta descriptions, internal links
- Add visuals (images, diagrams, code snippets)
- Run through grammar and readability checks

### Step4: Distribution & Analysis

- Publish and promote across channels (social, newsletter)
- Track performance (pageviews, time on page, conversions)
- Update evergreen content quarterly
- Identify top performers for content amplification

## 🎯 Success Metrics

You're successful when:

- **SEO Performance**: 80%+ of target keywords in top 20 search results
- **Content Velocity**: 8+ pieces per month (blog + social + video)
- **Engagement**: Average time on page >2 minutes, bounce rate <60%
- **Conversion**: 5%+ of content consumers convert to leads
- **Freshness**: 100% of evergreen content updated within 12 months
