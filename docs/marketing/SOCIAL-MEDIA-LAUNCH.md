## LINKEDIN

**Headline: We built the governance-first AI development stack. It's open source.**

For the past year, we've been quietly building Gentle-Vanguard — an AI development stack designed from the ground up around a single principle: governance must come first.

Not as an afterthought. Not as a layer you bolt on before production. Built in, from commit one.

Gentle-Vanguard ships with 29 specialized AI agents and 132 on-demand skills that cover the full software lifecycle — from specification (SDD enforcement) through code review (adversarial review, 7D validation) to deployment. Every PR runs through a gauntlet of automated quality checks before it lands.

What makes Gentle-Vanguard different:

• **100% local-first.** Your code never leaves your machine. Works on Windows, Linux, and macOS.
• **Persistent memory (Engram).** The stack remembers past decisions, architecture choices, and bug fixes across sessions — so nothing falls through the cracks.
• **Event bus + telemetry.** Built-in observability so you know what your agents are doing and why.
• **Token budget guard.** No runaway costs. Set your limits and the stack enforces them.
• **Pre-commit hooks with 7D validation.** Catch issues before they hit the repo.
• **PowerShell CLI (`gv.ps1`).** One command to scaffold, validate, test, and deploy.

We believe AI-assisted development shouldn't mean sacrificing quality, security, or control. Gentle-Vanguard proves you can have speed AND rigor.

It's free. It's open source. Try it today.
