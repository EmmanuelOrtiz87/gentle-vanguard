# LinkedIn - Auto-Delegation Deep Dive

How Foundation selects the right agent:

When you describe your task, the orchestrator analyzes:
- Keywords
- Context from Engram
- Complexity

Then routes to:
- explore - codebase research
- sdd-spec - specifications  
- sdd-apply - implementation
- verify - testing

Instead of one AI doing everything poorly, you get specialists.
