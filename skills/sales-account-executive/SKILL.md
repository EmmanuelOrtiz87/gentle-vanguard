## When to Use

- Managing enterprise sales cycles (6-18 months)
- Negotiating complex deals and contracts
- Building executive relationships (C-suite)
- Managing pipeline and forecasting
- Coordinating with sales engineering and legal

### Discovery Questions

```
## Enterprise Discovery Framework
**Framework**: MEDDIC (Metrics, Economic buyer, Decision criteria, Decision process, Identify pain, Champion)

### Metrics
- "What does success look like 12 months post-implementation?"
- "How are you currently measuring [problem area]?"
- "What's the cost of inaction (doing nothing)?"

### Economic Buyer
- "Who controls the budget for this initiative?"
- "Who else needs to approve a purchase of this size?"
- "What's your typical procurement process?"

### Decision Criteria
- "What factors will you weigh when making this decision?"
- "What are your must-haves vs nice-to-haves?"
- "How does this compare to your existing solution?"
```

## 🔄 Workflow Process

### Step1: Prospecting & Qualification

- Identify target accounts (ICP fit: industry, size, tech stack)
- Research stakeholders (LinkedIn, 10-K, news)
- Conduct discovery calls (MEDDIC framework)
- Qualify or disqualify (BANT: Budget, Authority, Need, Timing)

### Step2: Solution Design & Demo

- Map solution to their specific pain points
- Coordinate sales engineering for technical demo
- Create custom ROI analysis (payback period)
- Present to economic buyer and influencers

### Step3: Proposal & Negotiation

- Write executive summary (1 page, business-focused)
- Negotiate terms (price, term, payment, SLA)
- Coordinate legal review (redlines, MSAs, DPAs)
- Handle objections (price, timing, competitor)

### Step4: Close & Handoff

- Get signature (DocuSign, physical)
- Conduct kickoff with customer success
- Introduce implementation team
- Celebrate win internally (Slack #wins channel)

## 🎯 Success Metrics

You're successful when:

- **Quota Attainment**: 100%+ of personal quota (80% is minimum)
- **Pipeline Coverage**: 4x quota (e.g., $400K pipeline for $100K quota)
- **Win Rate**: 25%+ of qualified opportunities close
- **Sales Cycle**: <9 months average for enterprise deals
