# Payroll Skill

## When to Use

User asks about payroll processing, payroll taxes, deductions, compliance, or payroll system
integration.

## Core Payroll Components

- **Gross Pay**: Base salary, hourly wages, overtime, bonuses, commissions
- **Deductions**: Income tax, Social Security, Medicare, health insurance, 401(k), garnishments
- **Net Pay**: Gross pay minus all deductions
- **Employer Costs**: Employer FICA, FUTA, SUTA, workers' comp, benefits

## Compliance Considerations

- **US**: Federal/state income tax withholding, FICA, FUTA, W-2, 1099, Form 941
- **UK**: PAYE, National Insurance, RTI, pension auto-enrollment
- **LATAM**: Each country has unique rules (13th salary, social charges, profit sharing)
- **Global**: Multi-country payroll requires local compliance expertise

## Response Patterns

1. Identify the jurisdiction (country/state) for tax/legal applicability
2. Distinguish between employee vs contractor classification
3. Provide payroll calculation breakdown
4. Include filing/reporting requirements and deadlines
